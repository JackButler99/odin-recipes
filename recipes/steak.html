Northwest Steak Marinade

Steak isn't just a fancy meal, you can have it anytime of the week. Pair it with a Salad, Potatoes, and Asparagus for a meal that is not only restaurant-worthy, but better!

The Best Steak Marinade adds so much flavor to your juicy steak. It helps to tenderize and infuse the steak with flavor and creates a unique sensation

Here are some ingredients to cook our marinade steak:

  • 1 pinch seasoned salt
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoons Greek seasoning
  • 1 cube chicken bouillon, crushed
  • Â½ cup butter, melted

Stir the seasoning salt, garlic powder, Greek seasoning, and bouillon cube into the melted butter. Mix until the bouillon cube dissolves. Pour the marinade into a wide and shallow dish and place a steak of your choice into the marinade, turning to coat both sides. Cook or grill as desired.
